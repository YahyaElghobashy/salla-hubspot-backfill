Set up · orders sync

1 · Connect HubSpot & relay secret

The HubSpot private-app token needs CRM read/write scopes (contacts, orders/commerce, line items, custom objects). Secrets are stored only in a local .env and never shown again.

2 · Salla relay

Import relay/relay_blueprint.template.json into Make, select your Salla connection, turn it ON, paste the webhook URL. The test pulls your store's order-status list through it.

3 · Google Sheets & Drive (optional)

Live audit rows and order-JSON archive. Skip it and the engine keeps complete local CSV mirrors instead. Needs credentials.json (OAuth desktop client) next to serve.py.

4 · Pipeline mapping

Pick the order-pipeline stage used at creation, then map each Salla status to a stage. Unmapped statuses fall back to the default stage.

5 · Window, pacing & save

The engine sweeps this window in fixed slots, oldest first, and keeps its position in cursor.json. Stop and resume any time; deduplication makes rescans free.

Lanes share pacing globally — N lanes never exceed one lane's budget. If other integrations search the same portal, lower the search limit to your fair share of the 5/s pool.
